Platform Overview

Our educational platform is designed to support a wide range of educational activities, offering tools and resources tailored to the needs of Admins, Teachers, and Students.

Roles and Responsibilities

Admin

  • Create and manage teacher accounts
  • Promote or demote users between student and teacher roles
  • Edit user information
  • Send email notifications with login credentials

Teacher

  • Create and manage classes
  • Develop and manage lessons
  • Create fill-in-the-blank, true/false, and multiple-choice questions
  • Assign students to classes and provide answers for questions
  • View student results for lessons and questions

Student

  • Attempt questions within lessons
  • Receive automatic scoring and feedback
